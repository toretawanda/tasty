The Restaurant

Here at Tasty we strive to deliver quality food that brings a smile to the face. We are a café-restaurant that believes that a tasty meal can make the day much more brighter. I mean, who doesn't want to have a great tasting breakfast or lunch?

Our founder, John Smith, always had a dream to serve a smile through good food at his own café one day. Through his hard work and determination he was able to turn his dream into reality, naming his café Tasty to serve as a reminder of his goal.
