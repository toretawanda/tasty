The Restaurant

Mshengu story begins in 1995 when the very first restaurant opened in a small retail space in Malamulele, South Africa, but the stage was set long before…!

The Mediterranean spirit of generosity, quality ingredients and simplicity inspire our menu, inviting you to experience the fabulous flavours of the local with friends and family

Menu For Today

Today’s Menu was born out of a love of food. Determined to create higher quality frozen healthy prepared meals was our founder's "field of dreams". If he made it, they would eat it. Our team of highly talented and passionate chefs have been creating a real food menu since 2002. Using the highest quality ingredients to ensure you enjoy every bite.
