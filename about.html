Where we come from, and where we're going ->

Our History

Our founder, John Smith, was born and bred here in South Africa to caterers Peter and Sarah Smith. During his younger years, he always admired his parents and their cooking, the way their customers always smiled after eating a meal. This inspired his dream to serve a smile to people with the taste of his food.

Following his dream, he started working as a waiter at a small café in Johanesberg, Derick's Coffee. There he worked until he matriculated and went on to study at The Wooden Spoon cooking school. After his studies, he returned to Derrick's Coffee Shop to catch up, only to find that it had closed. Going to the bank, John took out a loan and bought the old café - reforming it into Tasty.

Now, 10 years later, Tasty has become a becon of good food and comfort, one that aims to bring smiles the same way John's parents did.
